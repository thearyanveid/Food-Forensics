Welcome to FoodForensics

FoodForensics is an intelligent food analysis platform designed to help consumers understand the nutritional quality and potential health risks of packaged foods.


By using technologies such as AI analysis, barcode scanning, and OCR image processing, our system extracts ingredient and nutrition information directly from product labels and evaluates them based on health standards.

Product Scanning

Upload food label images or scan barcodes to automatically extract ingredient and nutrition information using AI-powered OCR.

Health Risk Analysis

Our system evaluates products for high sugar, sodium, harmful additives, and other risk factors to generate a health risk score.

Ingredient Intelligence

Detect harmful preservatives, artificial colors, sweeteners, and allergens that may affect your health.

Personalized Insights

Get recommendations based on your dietary goals, allergies, and medical conditions.

Healthier Alternatives

Discover better product options with lower risk scores and healthier nutritional values.

Nutrition Tracking

Track your daily nutrient intake and monitor your health using AI-driven insights and analytics.
